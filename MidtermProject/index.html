About

Games

Play Snake

Use WASD or the arrow keys to move around.
Eat the red apples to grow.
Don't run into the walls or yourself!

Play Pong

Use W/S and the up/down arrow keys to move the paddles.
Try to bounce the ball between the paddles as long as you can!

Play Platform Jumper

Use A/D or the left/right arrow keys to move the block.
Jump on the platforms to keep from falling!
